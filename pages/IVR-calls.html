Interactive voice response (IVR) optimization

Reducing incorrectly placed customer support calls in the IVR by 17%

Key Moments

  • Consolidated existing data to frame the problem.
  • Conducted benchmark testing and validation of proposed solution with closed cardsorting tests.
  • Proposed small changes that had a big impact.

Tl;Dr

I audited customer calls to the IVR, interviewed stakeholders, conducted pre and post tests, and delivered a solution that reduced the overhead for our colleagues in the call center.

The Story

Revealing the problem

We collected and consolidated all data about inbound call categories for the previosu year, and interviewed key stakeholders. Results showed customers frequently chosing incorrect categories.

Designing tests

I created a closed cardsorting study in Optimal Workshop, lead it's validation, and gathered and analysed test results from the benchmarking test and the proposed changes.

Outcome

We proposed a streamlined IVR categorization system that could scale with product evolution while improving clarity for users.
